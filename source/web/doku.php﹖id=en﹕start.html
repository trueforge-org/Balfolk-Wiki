Welcome to the Dance Wiki!

This wiki contains information about different (balfolk) dances and their variations as well as tips for dancers, instructors and musicians. Everyone is welcome to improve and complete the information on this site. Before you need to create an account here. The original Dance Wiki was essentially maintained and hosted by the Gonnagles. Unfortunately their server is no longer available and that's why you are here now. Currently, most content is taken directly from the original Dance Wiki. This Dance Wiki is still under development and it is possible that some things change. If you have have suggestions for improvements and/or changes, please let us know! This can be done by sending an email to Balfolk Enschede, or by adding it to the requests page under improvements.
